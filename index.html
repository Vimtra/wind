About The Windgrove

The Windgrove is more than just a real estate project, it's a thoughtfully planned opportunity to own land in Kotagiri, one of the most serene and scenic hill stations in Tamil Nadu. Located 6,400 feet above sea level in the heart of the Nilgiri Hills, The Windgrove offers premium plots for sale in Kotagiri, just 30 km from Ooty and only 2.7 km from the picturesque Uyilatti Falls.

  • Prime Location – A scenic, high-potential investment destination.
  • Future-Ready Planning – Thoughtfully planned layouts for lifestyle and value.
  • High ROI Potential – Hill station plots with high growth potential.
  • Luxury in Nature – Premium community surrounded by nature.
